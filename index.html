About

I don’t wait for
the world to change.

I’m Hephzibah Ohene — a strategist, founder, and builder working across infrastructure, sustainability, and government in Ghana and West Africa. As Head of Government Relations & Special Projects at VAOB Infrastructure Group, I navigate the spaces where policy, capital, and delivery meet, turning complex mandates into real outcomes.

But my work doesn’t stop at a job title. I build companies. I originate deals. I connect the people who have the resources with the people who have the vision — and I make sure something tangible comes out the other side.

“My measure of success is simple: did something real get built?”

Trained in marketing strategy through the Chartered Institute of Marketing (UK), and currently deepening my expertise in sustainable development, I bring both commercial rigour and long-term thinking to everything I touch.
